What is minecraft?

Mine craft is a survival game where you have to find materials, craft tools fight entities, build shelters, find food, and defeat the final boss which is the ender dragon.

materials

Materials in this game or used to build shelter and make tools. materials can range wood all the way to netherite. the better the material the better you tools, some materials are also very rare and some are in a different dimension.

Tools

tools are the weapons or things you craft to get materials fight entities and protect yourself. certain tools have certain purposes. for example the pickaxe, the pickaxe is used for mining ores and the better the pickaxe the better the ores you can get.

Friendly mobs

friend mobs are mobs who will not hurt you for example villagers , animals etc... The villagers can be traded with and animals can be tamed for a farm or tamed for riding like a horse. Villagers can be found at villages and animals can be out in the wild

hostile mobs

Hostile mobs are mobs that will attack you these mobs are creepers, zombies,spiders,skeletons,enderman, and many more. some of these mobs have special things that are unique to them. for example the enderman, he is tall and can teleport, when killed there is a chance the the enderman will drop a ender pearl which when thrown will alllow you to teleport.
